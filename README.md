### Hi, I'm ØYNN 👋

I build small tools that fit in your menu bar and try to stay out of your way.

**[Sidefy](https://github.com/sidefy-team/sidefy)** — a macOS menu bar app that unifies Calendar, Reminders, RSS, GitHub, and custom data sources
into a live stream at the edge of your screen, with an open **[plugin repo](https://github.com/sidefy-team/sidefy-plugins)** to extend sources via JavaScript.

**[Locus Agent](https://github.com/sha2kyou/locusagent)** — a local-first desktop AI agent for macOS and Windows,
with workspace tools, MCP, memory, and scheduled tasks — all on your machine.
